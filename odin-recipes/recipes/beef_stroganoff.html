Beef Stroganoff

Description

This dish originates from Russia, but it has variations all over the globe. This particular recipe is an American style of Beef Stroganoff.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds (about 680 g) ground beef
  • 8 oz (about 22 g) fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups (about 354 ml) beef broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (about 354 ml / 368 g / 13 oz) sour cream
  • 3 cups hot cooked egg noodles

Steps

  1. Cook mushrooms, onions, and garlic in butter in a skillet over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until onions are tender, then remove from skillet.
  2. Cook beef in the same skillet until brown. Stir in 1 cup of beef broth, the salt, and the Worcestershire sauce. Heat to boiling, then reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Stir remaining 1/2 cup broth into flour, then stir into beef mixture. Add onion / mushroom mixture, then heat to boiling while stirring constantly for 1 minute. Stir in sour cream; heat until hot without boiling. Serve over hot noodles.
