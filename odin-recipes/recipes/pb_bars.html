Peanut Butter Bars

Description

These rich snacks require only a handful of ingredients, don't need to be baked, and can be prepared in about an hour!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (about 150-200g) graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups (about 250g) powdered sugar
  • 1 cup melted butter or margarine
  • 1 cup and 4 tablespoons (about 300ml) peanut butter
  • 1 & 1/2 cups (about 250g) semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs, powdered sugar, butter / margarine, and 1 cup peanut butter until well blended. Spread mixture into an ungreased shallow pan
  2. Melt chocolate chips and 4 tablespoons peanut butter in a microwave safe bowl or on the stovetop. Spread mixture on top of the graham cracker crust.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, then cut into squares.
