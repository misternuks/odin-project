Carbonara

Description

Carbonara spaghetti usually features eggs, hard grating cheese like pecorino romano or parmesan, pork, and pepper. Here is a simple yet delicious version of the Italian dish!

Ingredients

  • 150 g dried spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to packet instructions.
  2. Dice the pork and place in frying pan on medium heat (with olive oil if necessary). Add a pink of black pepper. Cook until golden and crispy, then turn off the heat.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then finely grate in the parmesan and mix well.
  4. Transfter the pasta into the frying pan and toss with the bacon
  5. Turn off the heat. Pour the parmesan egg mixture into the pan; keep mixing and adding pasta cooking water until the suce is sikly, ensuring the heat is never hot enough to scramble the eggs.
  6. Serve the pasta with an extra grating of parmesan (and an extra egg if desired).
